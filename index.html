Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order on KASH?

Ordering on KASH is simple! First, download our app from the App Store or Google Play. Browse through our categories or use the search function to find what you need. Add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout where you'll select your payment method and delivery address. Confirm your order, and you'll receive updates every step of the way until delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment options for your convenience: Mobile money (MTN, Orange, etc.), Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), Bank transfers, and KASH Wallet (our in-app payment system that lets you store funds for faster checkout). All payments are processed securely with encryption to protect your financial information.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the supplier's location. In urban areas, most orders arrive within 1-3 hours for express delivery or within 24 hours for standard delivery. For rural areas, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll see estimated delivery times during checkout and can track your order in real-time through the app.

Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return through the app within 7 days of delivery. The item must be in its original condition with all packaging intact. Some items like perishable goods or personalized products may not be eligible for return. Our customer support team can guide you through the return process if needed.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach us through: Live chat in the KASH app (fastest response), Email at support@kash.com, Phone at +237 676411380, or through our social media channels (@KASHSupport). For order-specific issues, please have your order number ready for faster resolution.
