Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order on KASH?

Simply download our app, browse products, add to cart, and checkout with your preferred payment method. It's that easy!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location but typically range from 1-3 business days within urban areas.

Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for most products. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

How do I become a supplier on KASH?

Visit our supplier portal on the website and complete the application form. Our team will review your application.
